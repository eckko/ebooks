  • Knowledge is not as much a prerequisisite to understanding as is commonly supposed.
    • We do not have to know everything about something in order to understand it.
    • Too many facts are often as much of an obstacle to understanding as too few.
  • Magazines, media and social media are so designed as to make thinking seem unnecessary.
    • The packaging is often done so effectively that the viewer, listener, or reader does not make up his own mind at all.
    • Instead, he inserts a packaged opinion into his mind.
